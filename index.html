Instructions

Create a stationary orbiter by clicking on the canvas while no orbiter is selected

Select an orbiter by clicking its stats at the table on the bottom.

Selected orbiters will be blue on the table and light green on the canvas

Create a moving orbiter by clicking on the cavas when an orbiter is selected.

Edit the stats of orbiters by selecting them, editing the values, and deselecting them

Delete an orbiter and all orbiters orbiting it by pressing "Kill"
